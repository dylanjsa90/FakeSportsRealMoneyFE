Fake Sports Real Money is a web application for Fantasy Sports commissioners who are tired of nagging the deadbeat members of their leagues to pay the league dues.

FSR$ allows Fantasy League commissioners to create leagues and add members to those leagues for league members with overdue balances, FSR$ will send daily reminders by text and/or email reminding them to pay.

How It Works

  • Create a League
  • Add the league members with the contact information
  • FSR$ will start sending payment reminders after the league starts for those with outstanding balances
  • Since its automatic, you don't have to feel bad about hounding those deadbeats
